# Aura

**Aura is the AI companion that knows you, tells you the truth, and can spin up a virtual company to ship what you're dreaming about.**

---

## What Aura Is

Aura is built on three pillars that work together. The **Truth-First Engine** is the operating principle — every output carries a confidence rating, Aura refuses to hallucinate, it matches intent instead of answering an adjacent question, and it refuses sycophantic filler. This is the DNA, not a feature. The **Personal Companion** is the always-free daily habit layer — chat with persistent, encrypted, user-controlled memory that compounds over time, plus tasks, projects, and daily plans extracted from natural conversation.

The **Virtual Company Engine** is the activation moment and the paid layer. Twelve agents across five phases (Discovery → Design → Planning → Implementation → Verification) run on top of the user's accumulated companion memory to produce a working app preview plus a governance bundle: a PRD, ADRs, test plan, threat model, deployment runbook, and GTM brief. Together, the three pillars turn Aura into something a solo technical founder can trust with their real company — not just a prompt toy.
